About us

who we are

Hanuman Prasad Gramin Vikash Seva Samiti (HPGVSS) is a Non-Government organization working in the rural area of Bihar since 1995 for holistic development of the disadvantaged community particularly for the welfare of children & women through giving voice to them in terms of advocating their issues and be a partner of Government and likeminded in reducing their problems. This organization has been implementing various projects for them such as formation of SHG, conducting special child labour school, self employment generation activities for youths and women, inculcating habit of using toilets through constructing toilets, sensitizing community on Disaster management and other social issues namely malnutrition, poor health, poor education quality etc. Since its inception the organization has been continuously executing the above developmental program through various promotional agencies, Government and Non-Government institutions. The promoter and chief of this organization is a well experienced resource person in entrepreneurship development and has conducted many training program on technology based rural entrepreneurship development since 15 years and he has a vast knowledge on health and sanitation issues. Together with having such an experienced he has a good liaising with local civil society organization in and nearby district as well as with block and district administration.

Vision

Develop a society where children, women and adolescent can be health and lead a better life (The organization envisages empowered women, adolescent and youths can contribute his role for nation building in terms of useful knowledge with emphasis on human resource and social development).

Mission

To empower women and adolescent in terms of status (social & economical) of controlling over the resources and access to services providing them an opportunity to enhance knowledge & skill through training.

Objectives

To setup new type of self-help groups with resource less rural people.
To develop the skills of the neglected sections of the society so that it serves as an income generation activity.
To make a Corpus Fund for groups & Community & provide them training.
To Provide support to resource less in all sectors.
To improve status & position of women & poor in society.
To promote community based programs.
To promote the interest of people in environment and sanitation.
To tender services to the women, children & youths of the country for their actual moral, social and legal rights.
To prepare people for natural disaster providing capacity building programs.
To create a sense of brotherhood, co-operation, mutual harmony, love and affection among the members and the general public.
To organize seminars & training workshop for the upliftment of the neglected section of the society.

Our Initiatives

This organization has been engaged implementing various projects related to the issues mentioning below:
Child protection: Special adoption agency(SAA) ,Child home, & child shelter through child line for missing children, child rights etc.
Health & Nutrition: Child health and nutrition through NRC & outsourced services in PHCs(NRC-Saharsa & Khagaria) and outsourced services in 2 PHCs of Saharsa),encouraging community on medicinal/herbal plants as well as on increasing dependency on organic fertilizers/Bio fertilizers inculcating habit of using smokeless chulha(Demonstrating).
Mother & child health: health services through mobile medical van & ultrasonography service in Sadar hospital of Muzaffarpur & outsourcing service in 2 PHC in Saharsa, Sensitizing them on health issues such as Kala-Azar, Hepatitis-B etc.
Sanitation: through construction of toilets and inculcating habit of hand washing among community and school children.
Disability: Establishing counseling cum rresource support & counseling centre
Education: Special school for children(as labour) and girls at child home.
Women empowerment & training: Awareness generation on social issues, Capacity enhancement of elected PRI(Women) and credit linkage establishment with banks.
Disaster risk reduction: providing training to PRIs, like minded organisation and sensitizing community on disaster issues and prevention in terms of risk reduction.
Hanuman Prasad Gramin Vikash Sewa Samiti (HPGVSS) implements social development programs ownself.the interventions implemented by HPGVSS vary from grassroot level to national level prorammes.we work with state government-Bihar , Government of Inida and several Charitable foundations. We are working in the 15 districts of Bihar and also we are working in the Assam state.
