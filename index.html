OUR INTERVENTION AREAS

HEALTH

From Mobile health units to prevention programs, HPGVSS Health interventions take a comprehensive approach to providing underprivileged rural healthcare.

DISABILITY

In the disability space, we seek solutions to provide all-encompassing support to people with all types of disabilities (likes speech, Autism, CP, Plegia, etc) across the country.

EDUCATION

We support formal & informal schools and ensures the quality of education is accessible to the children from underdeveloped areas of Rurals.

LIVELIHOOD

HPGVSS agricultural and non-agro-based livelihood programs are exploring the rural income-generating programs.

WATER & SANITATION

We are implementing projects for providing safe drinking water and sanitation facilities.

SKILL TRAINING

HPGVSS implementing skill projects for the skill up-gradation for the rural underprivileged youths. providing residential or non-residential training.
